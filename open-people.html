Open people

Our culture of openness around people and processes gives us a common understanding of what we value. It enables us to challenge and improve everything we do. Although these don't change as often as our source code, they do need to change as the team does.

Career Progression

We recognise people and give them a clear path to develop themselves at every stage of their career. We publish our career progression information both internally and externally so that people can advance their career regardless of their background.
