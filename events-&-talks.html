Events & Talks

Our developers can often be found talking about building our digital products at a number of different events and conferences. We even host a few events at our offices in London.

Events at the Guardian

FunctionalJS London

FunctionalJS is a meetup to learn about functional programming with JavaScript – whether that means using Underscore/Lodash, exploring currying and functional-reactive programming, or languages that compile to JavaScript to bring more functional tools to bear.

Girls in Tech London - 1st October

Girls in Tech London is the UK branch of the Girls in Tech Network. On 1st October the Guardian will be hosting the event with Chief Digital Officer, Tanya Cordrey, talking about the future of news.

London Elasticsearch User Group - 10th December

Mariot Chauvin and Jenny Sivapalan will be giving a talk on Getting it right, eventually - lessons learned from tuning Elasticsearch for 1000s of searches per second.
